Step-by-Step

Get Started in Minutes

No technical setup needed. Just open Telegram and follow these steps.

01

Open the Bot

Launch Telegram, search for @cucumverse_bot, and tap /start to initialize. The bot will greet you with wallet options.

02

Create or Import Main Wallet

Create a fresh wallet or import your existing one using your private key. This becomes your main wallet — the source of all funds.

⚠️ Never share your private key with anyone.
03

Generate Bot Wallets

Choose how many bot wallets to generate. Each wallet acts as an independent buyer. The bot provides a downloadable .txt backup file — save it in case of crashes or accidental deletion.

04

Distribute SOL to Bot Wallets

Send SOL from your main wallet to all bot wallets in one action. For example: distribute 0.5 SOL across 10 wallets = 0.05 SOL each. Check wallet balances anytime from the bot.

05

Configure Trading Settings

Set your trading parameters before launching: Slippage (e.g. 10% for faster fills), Min Buy (e.g. 0.01 SOL), Max Buy (e.g. 0.035 SOL), and Sell Percent — the profit % at which bots auto-sell. Always leave enough SOL per wallet for gas fees.

06

Start Trading

Hit Start Trading. The bot will ask you to paste a token contract address. Find a token on PumpFun, copy its address, and paste it — all bot wallets execute buys simultaneously, instantly.

✅ Non-sniper execution — undetectable on PumpFun.
07

Monitor Your Positions

View all bot wallet balances and token holdings from the bot at any time. Check the live token price and info of the token you're trading directly within the bot.

08

Sell & Collect Profits

Bots auto-sell at your set profit percentage, or you can trigger a manual sell across all wallets at once. Volume pumps, profits collect — all from one Telegram command.

🚀 Works for your own token or any PumpFun token.
